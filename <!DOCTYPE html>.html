Paper Airplane DIY

Steps How to Build the perfected paper Airplane!
Step One

    Step 1

    Fold the paper in half vertically.

      Be careful.
      Do not rip it.
      If ripped. Grab another paper.

Step Two

    Step 2

    Unfold the paper and fold each of the top corners into the center line.

      Be careful.
      Do not rip it.
      If ripped. Grab another paper.

Step Three

    Step 3

    Fold the top edges into the center line.

      Be careful.
      Do not rip it.
      If ripped. Grab another paper.

Step Four

    Step 4

    Unfold the paper and fold each of the top corners into the center line.

      Be careful.
      Do not rip it.
      If ripped. Grab another paper.

Step Five

    Step 5

    Fold the plane in half toward you.

      Be careful.
      Do not rip it.
      If ripped. Grab another paper.

Step Six

    Step 6

    Fold the wings down, matching the top edges up with the bottom edge of the body.

      Be careful.
      Do not rip it.
      If ripped. Grab another paper.
